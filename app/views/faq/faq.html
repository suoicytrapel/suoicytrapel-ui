Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

why should i look for event vendors online?

Today everyone is busy with their regular activities/work. So organising an event, lets say from daughter's birthday party to son's marriage, from parent's anniversary party to grandparent's retirement bash, it is not possible to meet every vendor and choose what is best. So Le Partycious provides you an online platform to choose from numerous listed vendors whichever suits you the best according to your budget and requirement.

Can i book the vendor online from Le Partycious website?

Currently Le Partycious is focusing on providing the maximum relevant information for each vendor listed, so that the information proves to be a choosing factor for any user. For now, LePartycious does not give the facility to book your vendor online, but based on your feedback and suggestions we can always work towards providing this facility.

Can LePartycious organise my event?

Yes, Le Partycious have its separate highly skilled event Management team, which organizes and manages your event to make sure that you enjoy every single bit of your event without bothering about anything. So you just have to call us or drop a mail give and we will be there for you in no time.

I am a vendor and i want to get myself registered on Le Partycious. How can i do so?

Just send a mail to us @ lepartycious@gmail.com with your telephone number and address in mail body and in no time we will be there for you.
