ABOUT LEPARTYCIOUS

Do you want to organize a surprise party on your anniversary but unable to do so because of your busy office hours?
Do you wish to plan a birthday party for your kid but got confused whether it will lie in your budget or not?
Want to enjoy your son's marriage without having any botheration to handle the event/guests yourself?

Planning for a party? Then you have come to the right place.

Tell us the way you want your party to be like and LePartycious will provide you with apt vendors that matches your requirements and budget.

In short, just pick us for managing your event and LePartycious will make it a memorable one for you.

Established in October 2015, LePartycious is an online platform for organising events.Which provides you with the relevant information regarding different venues, caterers, photographers, decorators and much more. Its all about bringing all the vendors under one roof so that they coordinate well and work towards a success story of an event. So precisely we are here to make your work convenient and hassel free by helping you choose from the diversified options available, that fits in your budget too.
